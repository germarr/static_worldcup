How Predictions Are Calculated

  • World Cup Win %: Each team's probability to win the tournament, from Kalshi prediction market data.
  • Log Ratio: ln(P_Home / P_Away) - measures the relative strength between teams.
  • Raw P(Home): Logistic function converts log ratio to a probability between 0 and 1.
  • Competitiveness: How close the match is expected to be. Closer matches have higher draw probability.
  • Draw Adjustment: Base draw rate (26%) scaled by competitiveness, with remaining probability split between win outcomes.
