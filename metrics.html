How to Read the Data

  • Win Prob %: The midpoint between bid and ask prices - represents the market's implied probability that this team wins the tournament.
  • Bid: The highest price someone is willing to pay for a "yes" contract (team wins).
  • Ask: The lowest price someone is willing to sell a "yes" contract.
  • Volume: Total number of contracts traded for this team.
